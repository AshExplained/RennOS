# Revenue Strategy Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Time period, specific goals, constraints
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Core brand identity
- `data/strategy/quarterly-roadmap.md` — Current roadmap
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience segments
- `data/finance/revenue-history.md` — Historical revenue data

## Steps

1. Read brand DNA, roadmap, audience personas, and revenue history
2. Research market trends and monetization models via web
3. Define the revenue strategy:
   - **Revenue goal** — Target for the period (with stretch goal)
   - **Income streams** — Which streams to invest in (ranked by potential)
   - **New streams to explore** — Untapped revenue opportunities
   - **Streams to cut** — Low ROI streams not worth the effort
   - **Pricing strategy** — Align with @pricing-strategist recommendations
   - **Revenue mix** — Target percentage per stream (diversification)
4. Align with quarterly roadmap milestones — what revenue milestones map to planned launches?
5. Identify dependencies (product launches, audience growth thresholds, partnerships needed)
6. Define success metrics and review cadence

## Output

- Write to `data/finance/revenue-strategy-[period].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with key strategic decisions
