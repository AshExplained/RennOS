# Community Design Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Community focus, target member, pricing model
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Core brand identity
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience segments

## Steps

1. Read brand DNA and audience personas
2. Research community models and platforms via web
3. Design the community:
   - **Value proposition** — Why would someone pay to be here?
   - **Target member** — Which persona, what they get that they can't get elsewhere
   - **Tier structure** — Free / paid / premium tiers with what's included at each level
   - **Content mix** — Exclusive content, AMAs, workshops, resources, templates
   - **Engagement mechanics** — Challenges, accountability partners, wins channel, weekly rituals
   - **Moderation plan** — Rules, enforcement, community culture
4. Design the member journey:
   - Onboarding experience (first 7 days)
   - Ongoing engagement loop (weekly/monthly rhythm)
   - Retention mechanics (what keeps people staying)
   - Graduation/upsell path (what's next for advanced members)
5. Platform recommendation and setup requirements

- Write to `data/product/community-design-[name]-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with community design patterns
