# Beta Plan Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Product name, beta scope, target tester count
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Core brand identity
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience segments
- `data/product/product-spec-*.md` — Product spec

## Steps

1. Read brand DNA, audience personas, and product spec
2. Design the beta program:
   - **Objective** — What are we testing? (product, pricing, UX, content quality)
   - **Tester profile** — Who makes an ideal beta tester? (persona match, engagement level, feedback capability)
   - **Tester count** — How many testers needed for meaningful feedback
   - **Recruitment plan** — How to find testers:
     - Community members (@community-manager, Dept 3)
     - Email list segment (@email-marketing-manager, Dept 5)
     - Social callout
   - **What testers get** — Free access, discount, recognition, early adopter status
   - **What we need from them** — Feedback surveys, usage data, testimonials
   - **Timeline** — Beta start, feedback checkpoints, beta end, launch decision
   - **Feedback collection method** — Surveys, interviews, usage analytics, NPS
3. Define success criteria — what results = ready to launch?
4. Define go/no-go decision framework

## Output

- Write to `data/product/beta-plan-[product]-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with beta program patterns
