# Community Plan Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Community focus, size, stage (planning/active/scaling)
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Core brand identity
- `data/product/community-design-*.md` — Community design from Product
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience segments

## Steps

1. Read brand DNA, community design from Product (if exists), and audience personas
2. Plan the community experience:
   - **Purpose** — What brings members together?
   - **Member profile** — Who is the ideal member?
   - **Content rhythm** — Weekly/monthly cadence (exclusive posts, resources, live events)
   - **Engagement mechanics** — Challenges, accountability groups, wins channel, introductions
   - **Events** — AMAs, workshops, guest sessions, co-working sessions
   - **Moderation** — Rules, tone, how to handle conflict
   - **Member journey** — New member → active member → power user → advocate
3. Define health metrics: active member %, engagement rate, retention, NPS
4. Identify content needs → coordinate with Content Creation (Dept 2) for exclusive content

## Output

- Write to `data/customers/community-plan-[name]-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with community management patterns
