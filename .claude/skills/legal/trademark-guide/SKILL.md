# Trademark Guide Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Specific asset to protect, or "general guide"
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Brand assets

## Steps

1. Read brand DNA for brand assets
2. Research trademark basics via web:
   - What can be trademarked (names, logos, taglines, sounds, colors)
   - Trademark classes relevant to the user's business
   - Filing process (USPTO for US, international considerations)
   - Common law vs registered trademark rights
   - Costs and timeline
3. Assess the user's trademark position:
   - What assets should be trademarked? (prioritized list)
   - Do any similar marks already exist? (basic clearance search)
   - What's the risk of NOT trademarking?
4. Provide a trademark action plan:
   - Priority 1: Register immediately
   - Priority 2: Register soon
   - Priority 3: Monitor for now
5. Include practical tips for trademark maintenance (use it, monitor it, enforce it)

## Output

- Write to `data/legal/trademark-guide-[date].md`
- **Disclaimer:** This is guidance, not legal advice. Consult a trademark attorney for filings.
- Update your MEMORY.md with trademark findings
