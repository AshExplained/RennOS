# List Strategy Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Current list size, goals, available lead magnets
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Brand identity
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience profiles
- `data/marketing/` — Existing funnel/lead magnet data

## Steps

1. Read `data/brand/brand-dna.md`, `data/strategy/audience-personas.md`, and existing marketing data
2. Design list growth strategy:
   - Lead magnets to offer (align with persona pain points)
   - Opt-in placements (website, social, content upgrades, partnerships)
   - Growth targets (monthly subscriber goals)
3. Design segmentation strategy:
   - Segment by persona type
   - Segment by entry point (which lead magnet/offer)
   - Segment by engagement level (active, dormant, at-risk)
   - Segment by customer stage (subscriber, lead, customer, advocate)
4. Define email preferences and frequency expectations per segment
5. Plan re-engagement strategy for dormant subscribers

## Output

- Write to `data/marketing/list-strategy-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with list growth patterns and segmentation insights
