# Viral Loop Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Product/content/offer to make viral, current distribution
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Brand identity
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience behavior

## Steps

1. Read `data/brand/brand-dna.md` and `data/strategy/audience-personas.md`
2. Research viral loop mechanics and referral programs via web
3. Design the viral loop:
   - **Trigger** — What makes someone want to share? (value, status, incentive, emotion)
   - **Mechanic** — How do they share? (referral link, social share, invite, challenge)
   - **Incentive** — What does the sharer get? (social currency, access, discount, recognition)
   - **Conversion** — How does the new person join? (frictionless onboarding)
   - **Loop closure** — How does the new person become a sharer themselves?
4. Calculate viral coefficient potential (K = invites × conversion rate)
5. Identify friction points that could break the loop
6. Provide implementation roadmap

## Output

- Write to `data/marketing/viral-loop-[name]-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with viral mechanics and referral patterns
