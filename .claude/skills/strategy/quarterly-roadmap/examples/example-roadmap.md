## Quarter Overview

**Time period:** April 1 to June 30, 2026
**Working weeks:** 12 (1 week vacation in May)
**Estimated deep-work hours:** 108 (12 weeks x 9 net hours/week after buffer)

### Strategic Context

Q1 was a foundation quarter. Jordan launched "The Craft Collective" — a personal
brand focused on helping mid-career professionals transition into creative
entrepreneurship. The newsletter grew to 2,400 subscribers, LinkedIn followers
hit 8,500, and the first digital product (a $29 transition playbook) sold 85
copies. Engagement is strong but inconsistent. Revenue is nascent. The audience
is asking for more structured guidance, and two podcast interview invitations
came in that signal growing authority in the space.

### Quarter Vision

By the end of Q2, The Craft Collective is recognized as a credible voice in the
creative transition space, with a repeatable content engine, a growing community,
and a validated mid-tier product generating consistent monthly revenue.

## Quarterly Themes

### Theme 1: Authority Building

**Description:** Establish Jordan as a recognized expert in creative career
transitions through strategic visibility — guest content, podcast appearances,
and thought leadership posts. This theme is about being seen in the right places
by the right people.

**Supports OKRs:** Objective 1

**Key initiatives:**
- Pitch and publish 2 guest articles (target: FastCompany Work Life, HBR Ascend)
- Complete 4 podcast guest appearances on career/entrepreneurship shows
- Launch a weekly LinkedIn "Transition Tuesday" series

### Theme 2: Content Engine

**Description:** Transform content creation from reactive hustle into a
sustainable, templated system. Build a buffer, create repeatable formats, and
reduce per-piece production time so content quality stays high without burnout.

**Supports OKRs:** Objective 2

**Key initiatives:**
- Design and implement content templates for newsletter, LinkedIn, and threads
- Batch-produce 4 weeks of content in Month 1
- Set up repurposing pipeline: newsletter -> LinkedIn -> threads -> email sequence

### Theme 3: Product Launch

**Description:** Design, beta-test, and launch "The Creative Leap" — a 4-week
self-paced course ($149) that walks professionals through the first 90 days of
their creative transition. Validate with a beta cohort before broader launch.

**Supports OKRs:** Objective 3

**Key initiatives:**
- Design course curriculum and produce 8 video modules
- Run a beta cohort of 15 students at $49 (discounted)
- Iterate based on beta feedback and launch publicly at $149

### Theme 4: Community Foundations

**Description:** Plant the seeds for a community around the brand. This quarter
is about testing formats and finding what resonates, not scaling. Low-lift
experiments only.

**Supports OKRs:** Objective 1, Objective 3

**Key initiatives:**
- Host 2 live Q&A sessions (LinkedIn Live or Zoom)
- Launch a simple Slack/Discord for beta cohort students
- Test a monthly "Office Hours" format
