### Escalation Protocol

- **Level 1 (Watch):** Elevated mentions, no media pickup. Continue monitoring.
- **Level 2 (Engage):** Growing mentions, media interest. Deploy public response.
- **Level 3 (Full Crisis):** Trending, mainstream media, stakeholder pressure. Activate full 72-hour protocol.
- **Level 4 (Critical):** Legal exposure, safety risk, existential brand threat. Engage legal counsel, consider external PR support.

### De-escalation Criteria

The crisis is stabilizing when:

- [ ] Mention volume has declined for 12+ consecutive hours
- [ ] Sentiment ratio is improving (neutral/positive growing)
- [ ] No new media outlets covering the story
- [ ] Direct stakeholder inquiries are decreasing
- [ ] No new amplifiers entering the conversation
