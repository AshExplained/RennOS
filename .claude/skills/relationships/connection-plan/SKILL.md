# Connection Plan Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Networking goal, niche, or specific people to target
- `data/personal/relationships/` — Contact data
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Audience segments
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Brand identity

## Steps

1. Read contact data, audience personas, and brand DNA
2. Research potential connections via web if targeting specific people or communities
3. Create a connection plan:
   - **Goal** — What the user wants from networking (partnerships, mentorship, community, growth)
   - **Target people** (5-10) — Name, why them, what mutual value exists
   - **Where to find them** — Events, platforms, communities, mutual connections
   - **Approach** — How to initiate (warm intro, cold outreach, engage their content first)
   - **Timeline** — When to reach out, follow-up cadence
4. Prioritize by mutual value × accessibility

## Output

- Write to `data/personal/relationships/connection-plan-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with networking patterns
