# Monetization Ideas Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Focus area or "general brainstorm"
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Brand positioning
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — What the audience wants
- `data/content/top-performers.md` — Content that signals product-market fit
- `data/finance/revenue-history.md` — Revenue context

## Steps

1. Read `data/brand/brand-dna.md`, `data/strategy/audience-personas.md`, `data/content/top-performers.md`, and `data/finance/revenue-history.md`
2. Research digital product trends and competitor monetization via web
3. Brainstorm 5-10 monetization ideas:
   - For each idea:
     - **Format** — Course, template pack, swipe file, community, membership, coaching, book, tool, SaaS
     - **Title/concept** — Specific name and positioning
     - **Target persona** — Which audience segment wants this
     - **Pricing model** — One-time, subscription, tiered
     - **Price range** — Based on market research and audience willingness to pay
     - **Production effort** — Low/medium/high + estimated timeline
     - **Revenue potential** — Conservative and optimistic estimates
     - **Existing assets** — What content/IP already exists that could be packaged?
4. Rank ideas by revenue potential × effort × brand fit
5. For the top 2-3 ideas, outline next steps to validate demand

## Output

- Write to `data/partnerships/monetization-ideas-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with monetization patterns and market insights
