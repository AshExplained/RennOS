# Social Video Plan Playbook

## Inputs

- $ARGUMENTS — Series theme, niche, platform target, or strategic goal
- `data/brand/brand-dna.md` — Brand voice and positioning
- `data/strategy/audience-personas.md` — Target audience profiles

## Steps

1. Read `data/brand/brand-dna.md` and `data/strategy/audience-personas.md`
2. Research successful video series formats via web:
   - What series structures work on the target platform
   - How top creators in the niche build recurring content
   - Optimal episode length and cadence for audience retention
3. Design the series:
   - **Series Name** — Memorable, branded, hashtag-friendly
   - **Consistent Hook Format** — Recognizable opening that viewers associate with the series (e.g., "Day [X] of...", "[Topic] in 60 seconds", "Things nobody tells you about...")
   - **Core Theme** — The through-line connecting all episodes
   - **Visual Consistency** — Recurring intro animation, color scheme, text style, thumbnail template
   - **Audio Consistency** — Signature sound, intro jingle, or music style
4. Plan 5-10 episodes:
   - Episode number
   - Title
   - Topic / angle
   - Hook (specific to this episode)
   - Target length
   - Key talking points or visual beats
5. Set publishing cadence (daily, 3x/week, weekly) based on production capacity
6. Identify 2-3 pilot episodes to produce first and test audience response
7. Define success metrics — views, saves, shares, follower growth, comments

## Output

- Write the series plan to `data/content/video/series-[name]-[date].md`
- Update your MEMORY.md with key findings and patterns discovered
