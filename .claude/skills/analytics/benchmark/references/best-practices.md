# Industry Benchmarks by Platform (2026)

> Last updated: March 2026
> Use these benchmarks as baselines when evaluating the user's performance. Always account for niche, audience size, and content format when comparing.

---

## 1. Twitter / X

### Engagement Rate
- **Average (all accounts):** 0.12%–0.50% (by followers); viral content can hit 3%–5%
- **Micro accounts (<5K followers):** 2%–5%
- **Mid-size accounts (5K–50K):** 1%–3%
- **Large accounts (50K+):** 0.5%–2%
- **Influencer average per post:** 0.39% (up from 0.09% prior year)
- Average reply rate: 0.02%–0.05%
- Average retweet rate: 0.5%–1%

### Impressions
- Active accounts average 50,000–200,000 organic impressions/month
- Platform saw a 50% increase in viewership in 2025, driven by viral moments

### Follower Growth
- Average weekly posting frequency rose from 15.97 (2024) to 17.34 (2025)
- Short-form video now surpasses text-based posts — 37% of users most likely to interact with short-form video from brands

### Trends
- Engagement declined 48% YoY across the platform (steepest of any major platform)
- Algorithm now favors video and threaded conversations over standalone text

## 2. Instagram

### Engagement Rate by Format
| Format | Engagement Rate (by followers) | Engagement Rate (by reach) |
|--------|-------------------------------|---------------------------|
| Reels | 1.48% | 3.31% |
| Carousels | 0.99% | 6.90% (highest) |
| Single images | 0.70% | 4.44% |
| Feed posts (avg) | 0.70% | — |

### Reach Rate
- **Reels reach rate:** 30.81% — more than 2x higher than carousels, images, or Stories
- 55% of Reels views come from non-followers (discovery engine)
- Reels get 36% more reach than carousels, but carousels earn 12% more engagement

### Overall Platform
- Instagram engagement rate: 0.48% (nearly flat YoY)
- Engagement declined 16% YoY (smallest decline among major platforms)

### Recommended Format Mix (2026)
- 60%–70% Reels (discovery)
- 20%–25% Carousels (engagement + saves)
- 10%–15% Stories (community + retention)

## 3. YouTube

### Click-Through Rate (CTR)
| Range | Rating |
|-------|--------|
| 2%–4% | Below average |
| 4%–7% | Average to good (most creators) |
| 7%–10% | Strong — outperforming most creators |
| 10%+ | Exceptional |

**CTR by content type:**
- Gaming: 8.5% (highest)
- Entertainment: 6%–7%
- How-to/Tutorial: 5%–6%
- Educational: 4.5% (lowest — more selective audience)

### Audience Retention
- **Long-form videos:** Target 50%+ average percentage viewed (APV)
- **Shorts:** Target 75%+ APV
- Retention above 50% is considered healthy across most content types
- Videos with strong retention past 60 seconds see higher comment rates and algorithmic promotion

### Subscriber Growth
- Growth is driven by repeatable systems: strong packaging (thumbnails + titles), compelling intros (first 30 seconds), and series formats
- Return viewer rate is now the most important growth predictor
- "Quality CTR" is a key algorithm signal — high CTR + low early retention triggers demotion

### YouTube Ads (CPM)
- Average YouTube Ads CPM: $3.53

## 4. LinkedIn

### Engagement Rate
- **Average across all industries:** 2.1%–5.20% (varies by measurement methodology)
- 8% YoY increase in engagement
- Comments have 15x more algorithmic weight than likes
- Dwell time drives 15.6% engagement vs 1.2% without

### Engagement by Content Format
| Format | Avg Engagement Rate |
|--------|-------------------|
| Native documents (PDFs) | 7.00% (14% YoY increase) |
| Carousels | 6.60% (highest interactions) |
| Video | 2%–5% |
| Images | 2%–5% |
| Text-only | 0.5%–2% |

Carousels generate 278% more engagement than video, 303% more than images, and 596% more than text-only posts.

### Engagement by Audience Size
- 1K–5K followers: 4%–7% is good
- 5K–50K followers: 2%–4% is good
- 50K+ followers: 1%–2% is a strong professional benchmark

### Reach & Impressions
- Impressions declined 63%–66% since 2023 (algorithmic shift)
- Platform now rewards quality interactions over volume

### LinkedIn Ads
- Average CPC: $5.50–$8.50
- Average CPM: $30–$50
- Average CTR: 0.44%–0.65%
- Average conversion rate: 2.0%–3.5%
- Most expensive major ad platform, but strongest B2B targeting

## 5. TikTok

### Engagement Rate
- **Average:** 3.70%–4.9% (highest of any major platform, up 49% YoY)
- <5K followers: ~4.2%+ typical
- 1M+ followers: 2.8%–3.5% typical

### Engagement by Content Niche
| Niche | Avg Engagement Rate |
|-------|-------------------|
| Fashion & clothing | 9.8% |
| Educational | 9.5% |
| Entertainment | 9.45% |

### Views & Growth
- 3% YoY growth in video views
- 45% YoY increase in shares per post
- Average comments per post fell 24% (shift toward passive engagement)

### Retention Benchmarks
- Target 40%–60% completion rate for sustained FYP exposure
- Strong retention past 60 seconds drives higher comment rates and algorithmic promotion regardless of video length

### TikTok Ads
- Average CPC: $0.20–$2.00
- Average CPM: $4.80–$9.16
- Average CTR: 0.61% (good range: 0.5%–1.5%)
- Conversion rate: 0.5%–5% (higher in e-commerce and fashion)

### Open Rates by Industry (Top Performers)
| Industry | Open Rate |
|----------|-----------|
| Government | 30.5% |
| Nonprofits | 25.2% |
| Education | 23.4% |

### Click Rates by Industry (Top Performers)
| Industry | Click Rate |
|----------|-----------|
| Legal | 4.90% |
| Manufacturing | 4.22% |
| Media | 4.10% |
| Overall range | 0.83%–4.90% |

### Important Context
- **Apple Mail Privacy Protection** automatically marks emails as "opened" for Apple Mail clients (46% of all email clients), inflating open rate metrics

- Segmented campaigns consistently outperform unsegmented by 2x–3x on CTR

## 7. Blog / Website

### Traffic Benchmarks
- Businesses with blogs generate 55% more website visits than those without
- Consistent publishing (15–20+ posts/month) can scale organic impressions significantly
- Organic search remains the largest traffic driver for content-led brands

### Time on Page
- **2026 benchmark:** 3 minutes 47 seconds average
- Interactive content pushes past 5 minutes
- Users who scroll 50%+ of page and spend 2+ minutes indicate genuine interest

### Bounce Rate
| Content Type | Bounce Rate Range |
|-------------|------------------|
| All sectors (average) | 41%–55% |
| B2B websites | 40%–60% |
| Blog / content sites | 70%–90% (normal — users find answers and leave) |
| Content pages (2026 avg) | 45% |

### Context
- Bounce rate above 70% on non-blog pages may signal search intent mismatch
- High blog bounce rate is not inherently negative if content answers the query
- Focus on engagement metrics (scroll depth, time on page) alongside bounce rate
